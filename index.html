About Us

Lemon Agro Farm is a sustainable farming initiative located in Pokhara, Nepal.

Mission & Vision

Mission: To promote sustainable agriculture, support local farmers, and provide hands-on learning opportunities for WWOOFers.

Vision: To create a global community focused on organic farming, environmental conservation, and food sovereignty.

Key Features

  • Organic Farming: Cultivation of lemons, oranges, strawberries, and other fruits/vegetables using organic methods.
  • WWOOFing Program: Hosting volunteers from around the world to learn and work on the farm.
  • Support for Local Farmers: Collaborating with local farmers to bring their products to global markets.

WWOOFing at Lemon Agro Farm

Join our global community of volunteers to learn and work on our organic farm.

What is WWOOFing?

WWOOFing is a global movement where volunteers (WWOOFers) work on organic farms in exchange for food, accommodation, and hands-on learning experiences.

What We Offer to WWOOFers

  • Hands-on Experience: Learn organic farming and sustainable practices.
  • Cultural Exchange: Experience Nepali culture and share your own.
  • Travel and Exploration: Explore the beauty of Nepal with fellow WWOOFers.
  • Home Away from Home: Comfortable accommodation and a family-like environment.
  • Camping and Night Stays: Enjoy camping, bonfires, and local-style meals.

About Pokhara

Known as the beautiful city of Nepal, Pokhara is surrounded by stunning landscapes and natural beauty. It's the perfect setting for our sustainable farming initiative.

Getting Here

Pokhara is approximately 200 km west of Kathmandu. You can reach here by:

  • Flight: 30 minutes from Kathmandu
  • Bus: 6-7 hours from Kathmandu
  • Private vehicle: 4-5 hours from Kathmandu
